donkpocket
/obj/item/reagent_containers/food/snacks/donkpocket

Donk Pockets are a food item that is a reference to a mass market brand of turnovers. They come with various "fillings" and can be cooked (or self-heat in some cases) to cause them to temporarily become more useful.

Vars

can_self_heatWhether the donk pocket is able to be made hot without a microwave by using it in-hand
filling_optionsThe reagents to be added to the donk pocket when it is initialized
hot_reagentsThe reagents to be added to the donk pocket when it is made hot (and removed when made cold)
is_hotWhether the donk pocket is currently hot. Hot donk pockets have additional reagents
was_heatedWhether the donk pocket was heated up already. Reheating does not re-add the extra reagents.
