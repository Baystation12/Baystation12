04 August 2022

AzlanonPC updated:

  • you can now powder pills with anything sharp, or your ID.
  • with thalers, straws or rolling paper, you can snort these to have them enter your bloodstream directly. Adherents, Dionae, robots and FBPs without a mouth can't do this.
  • Most drugs dont cause moaning, screaming, giggling or laughing at random anymore. Those who still do, are much subtler, or require higher doses. Some have received different effects like stuttering instead.
  • they have also received unique messages for the person when you are under their effect, and withdrawal.
  • Lactic acid is no longer referred to as lactate.

  • Unban panel and player panel inactivity timers should both actually be fixed now (Take 2).

Ryan180602 updated:

  • Removes most vendors on Torch
  • Prices of Food/Replacement vendors increase.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Doors should no longer allow mobs to enter their turfs while doing the close animation.
  • Janitor borgs can no longer delete holograms and other things that use effects overlays.

  • The old lab away site's window shutters are no longer see through.

  • Var-editing a ship's vessel_mass var is restricted to positive, non-zero numbers.
  • Player panel now displays how long a player's been logged out for.
  • Player panel inactivity time now converts to minutes, hours, etc as applicable.

30 July 2022

AzlanonPC updated:

  • Crossbow, zipgun and coilgun assemblies have been condensed into a single stock, able to be made from any rigid material (and still wood)
  • Department tape and wire now serve to tape makeshift guns together. Making a barrel out of aluminum cans is also possible, to replace pipes.
  • Crossbow assembly first step now requires a light switch as a trigger.
  • You can now fabricate soda can IEDs with some wire and welding tool tanks. Put coins and other small, metallic items inside to increase the shrapnel amount.
  • You can now create makeshift swords out of large metal blades and some imagination.
  • coilguns now need 2 magnetic coils instead of 3, along with a rail. Make one by assembling a wired rod and a rod together.
  • You can now craft trusses out of wood.
  • Leather can be used to craft belts, holsters and sheaths.
  • You can craft makeshift armor out of two rail assemblies and some metal sheets. For the aspiring mutineer.
  • Zipguns now have an unloaded visual.
