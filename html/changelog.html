01 April 2022

Mucker updated:

  • Added meteor buildmode, for the aspiring badmin.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Added a painting build mode. This currently only handles base colors and not decals such as floor tile decals or door stripes.
  • Gas scanner UI for both the scanner device and PDA module has been tweaked. Results no longer appear in chat and instead appear in the scanner's display, which will automatically open/update every time a scan is run. The display will also now display all information instead of being divided between 3 different 'Modes'. NOTE: The PDA module will not automatically open the program if it is not already open, but will update the UI if the window is already open.
  • Gas scanner PDA modules now work on turfs.
  • God mode mobs are no longer affected by explosions, fire, emp, or bullets.

24 March 2022

updated:

  • Allows the specialised arm/legguards to be removed and attached at will.
  • Removes slowdown from legguards, add slowdown to specialised plate carriers only.

Kell-E updated:

  • Clusterbangs now properly spawn their sub-grenades
  • Removes the marksman energy rifles ability to instantly knockdown it's victims
  • Increases the number of shots the marksman energy rifle has from 4 to 8
  • All kinds of fragmentation grenades are now significantly more lethal
  • Inaccurate shots are more likely to scatter and hit random locations upon their victim rather than their intended target.

Spookerton updated:

  • Donk pockets are more interesting. There are protein / vegetable / fruit / dessert / premium versions, with reagent contents variations for each.
  • Donk pocket boxes are more interesting. There are fixed contents boxes for each variation, and random mixed boxes.
  • Sin pockets have been replaced with premium donk pockets.
  • You can order donk pockets from supply. Ordering premium donk pockets is locked behind wire hacks.
  • Premium donk pockets have slightly less bonus and lots less malus.
  • Premium donk pockets are skrell-safe.
  • Re-heating donk pockets does not re-add their extra reagents. Finish your meal!
  • You can no longer use a microwave to create donk pockets from dough and meatballs.
  • Animal cubes expanding inside people have a short message sequence. They also newly guarantee fractures and internal organ damage.
  • Added Simulate Distant Explosion to the Fun tab. Plays the distant explosion sound for players in the admin's z group and optionally knocks them over.
