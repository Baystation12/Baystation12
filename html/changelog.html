  • Spider webs and cocoons now die properly.
  • Eggclusters now release some spiderlings when they are broken. The number of spiderlings released depends on how mature the egg cluster was.
  • Replicators now provide a replicator circuit board when dismantled instead of an auto lathe board.

15 December 2022

Spookerton updated:

  • IV stands and roller beds use IV bag transfer rates and allow setting their currently hung bag rate. IV bags can be set to 2, 1, REM, or 0.
  • It's easier to mess up with IVs. With less than basic medicine you'll be slow and likely to stab people by accident on rollers and stands. IV bags want trained instead.
  • You can hook up IV bags to people and squeeze them every three seconds to force in extra fluid. Harm intent is painful but pushes an extra three times the transfer rate. Other intents only push twice as much, but also only itch.
  • Setting up and collapsing roller beds takes a few seconds. The horror.
  • You can iron people on ironing boards. They sizzle. Also implements the fancy ironing board icon.
  • Roller beds and ironing boards with mobs buckled to them become turf-level mouse opaque. Clicking the mob will still interact with the mob, but you can click anywhere on the turf to interact with the bed / board.
  • Mapped blood pack distribution changed to include less nanoblood (but still some) and a mixture of other bloods.
  • IV bag contents is indicated with labels. You can remove the labels. Isn't that lovely?

12 December 2022

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Hand labelers will now skip all other interactions except labeling while turned on. This means they won't toggle locker locks or any other weirdness while also attaching labels. While the labeler is turned off, it will go into containers and behave as usual.
  • Hand labelers will no long turn on if you provide an invalid or empty string as the label text.
  • Added codex mechanics to hand labelers.
  • Default ship sensor range overheat threshhold reduced to 3, bringing it back in line to previous values. This allows upgrading the sensors with better manipulators to become viable. Maximum possible upgraded range before overheating is 6.
  • Ship sensor range inputs now only accept whole numbers, and will round any decimal value entered.
  • The weak variant of ship sensors has been removed. The GUP's sensors are now equivalent to all other sensors.
  • Raider and ninja now have aluminum and plastic added to their autolathes.
  • Crashed survivor pod and colony sites now have suit cooling units.
  • Luminol now properly reveals cleaned blood decals, unless they were cleaned with sterilizine.

05 December 2022

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Area boundaries for deck 1 and deck 3 aft hallways have been pushed back to line up with the physical boundaries created by the doors and windows in the hallways.
  • Disassembling racks no longer gives you free aluminum.
  • Guest pass terminals now allow guest passes to have a time limit of 60 minutes, up from 30 minutes.
  • You can now link PDAs to fax machines to receive notifications of received faxes from anywhere on the ship. You can do this by clicking the fax machine with your PDA.
  • You can no longer create new inflatables by spam clicking them.
  • Bunny slippers can now be found in the footwear section of character loadout.
  • Titanium (And other materials with high crafting costs) can now have their crafting menus opened again.
  • Claymores now use the correct material when they are crafted.
  • Mininghome now includes ground ores for the big drill.
  • Medbay has nanoblood again.
  • Prebuilt mechs, including the cargo powerloader, now have valid cameras in their sensors.
