23 July 2022

Spookerton updated:

  • Cheese is now fresh, aged, or blue. Making cheese makes fresh cheese, microwaves are used to turn that into either aged or blue. Aged and blue cheese have smells. All cheese types work with existing recipes interchangeably.

Mucker, sprites by Azlan updated:

  • Adds a couple of new admin-spawnable meteor variants for extreme destruction.
  • Adds the 'Bombard Z-Level' verb to make spontaneous and random explosions over a zlevel, or connected zlevels.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Explosion damage handling for walls and other standardized health atoms has been tweaked. Overall, explosions are now more damaging in general, and walls using plasteel as a material are now far less resilient to explosions than they used to be, while still being about 1/3 - 1/2 stronger. See https://github.com/Baystation12/Baystation12/pull/32256 for specific numbers.

SierraKomodo updated:

  • The red, yellow, and green indicator lights on gas canisters now update at roughly 30% and 60% of the effective maximum pressure (15,000 kPa). This means that canisters will show red if below ~5,000, yellow if below ~10,000, and green above those values.
