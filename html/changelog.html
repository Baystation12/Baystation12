18 May 2024

Spookerton updated:

  • Shards have different crossing sounds.
  • Crossing shards hurts simple mobs.
  • Crossing shards damages bare robotic and crystal organs.
  • If prone, shards can hurt any part of the body. Thick clothing protects against this.
  • If a shard hurts a meaty organ, it can embed. Different shard types have their own probability.
  • Shard damage is based on force instead of fixed.
  • Added caltrops. Caltrops are uplink-buyable shards that come in packs of 4 and pierce many types of shoe. Only magboots, regular (not toeless) workboots, combat boots, and antagonist galoshes protect against them.
  • Added thumbtacks in small containers next to every noticeboard. Thumbtacks are an offense against goodness that respect shoes and thick clothing but always embed and don't weaken, allowing the unwary to collect quite a few if moving unprotected.
  • There are 6 tacks per pot instead of 16.
  • Pill bottles can hold tacks.
  • Some mob types (mechs, silicons, bots) are not damaged by shards.
  • Caltrops are less good.
  • Osmium is heavier than platinum.
