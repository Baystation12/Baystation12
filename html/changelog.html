  • Scanner modules added for modular computers. Available (job-depending) on spawn, and also from supply crates.

  • If a scanner and a nanoprinter is installed, the printer can now only be refilled with blank paper.
  • The tax program should no longer produce duplicate copies or unexpected behavior.
