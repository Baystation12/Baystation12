  • Exoplanet flora now only will ever consume and exude gasses already present in the planet's atmosphere. No more plants pumping phoron into what should have been a habitable world.

Mucker updated:

SierraKomodo updated:

  • Borgs/robots/etc no longer have cameras. This means camera monitoring programs can no longer be used to track borgs, and AI cores cannot use borgs to gain vision where they otherwise can't see.
  • The spin-view verbs have been removed due to how broken overlays and effects become after using them.

AzlanonPC updated:

  • prosthetic organs now have bone-adjacent structures; Broken in similar ways, and repaired through robotic surgery.

  • Robot analyzers now show damage to robot brains and MMIs. Also do sound.
  • Robotic and crystalline parts now have custom broken names.
  • microbatteries now have 1000w cell, instead of 3000w. The cost of moving and power damage received by EMPs adjusted accordingly.

AzlanonPC updated:

  • coilguns now need 2 magnetic coils instead of 3, along with a rail. Make one by assembling a wired rod and a rod together.
  • You can now craft trusses out of wood.
  • Leather can be used to craft belts, holsters and sheaths.
  • You can craft makeshift armor out of two rail assemblies and some metal sheets. For the aspiring mutineer.
